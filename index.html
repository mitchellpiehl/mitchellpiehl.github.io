Welcome!

Hello and welcome to the site on the history of AI! This website will contain all you need to know about important and significant events, people, and papers published throughout the history of Artificial Intelligence.

Explore the timeline to view an interactive display on all the events with pictures, details, and links. Go to the people tab to learn all about the significant figures that have made artificial intelligence what it is today. View the papers section to learn everything you need to know about the most influential papers that have revolutionized the industry. The further readings tab will provide the literature where most of this content was gathered, and will include any recommended readings to learn more about the topic of the history of artificial intelligence. The online resources page will provide you with a bunch of valuable and fun webpages for you to further explore these historical developments and gather an appreciation of everything that goes into AI. Finally, the fundamentals page will explain all the fundamental concepts that go into Artificial Intelligence, from mathematical foundations such as Boolean Logic to important neural network architectures such as the transformer.

Enjoy exploring!
