The Worlds I See

Author: Fei Fei Li

Autobiography of the life of Dr. Fei-Fei Li, the founding director of the Stanford Institute for Human-Centered Artificial Intelligence. Goes into fine detail of how ImageNet was created, as well as the motivation behind Human-Centered AI, and AI4All. Talks about life growing up in China then moving to America as well as the big AI developments that happen during that time.
