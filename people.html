John McCarthy

John McCarthy was a computer scientist and cognitive scientist known as the father of AI. He coined the term "Artificial Intelligence" and developed LISP, a key programming language for AI research.

Marvin Minsky

Marvin Minsky was a cognitive scientist and co-founder of the MIT AI Lab. He made fundamental contributions to AI, cognitive psychology, and robotics.

Alan Turing

Alan Turing was a mathematician and logician who laid the foundations of modern computing. His Turing Test remains a key concept in AI philosophy.

Geoffrey Hinton

Geoffrey Hinton is a pioneer of deep learning and neural networks. His work on backpropagation revolutionized machine learning.

Yann LeCun

Yann LeCun developed convolutional neural networks (CNNs), which have been crucial for advancements in computer vision.
